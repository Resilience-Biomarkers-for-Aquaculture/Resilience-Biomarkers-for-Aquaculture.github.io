PERSONAL SUMMARY

I am a Systems Biologist with 8+ years of experience in 'omics' technology development and application. My main interests are in research and public education outreach on how organisms’ molecular networks respond to human-influenced environmental change. I am currently a Postdoctoral Research Associate in the lab of Steven Roberts in the School of Aquatic and Fishery Sciences at the University of Washington researching how ocean acidification impacts the physiology and epigenetic variation in bivalves.
